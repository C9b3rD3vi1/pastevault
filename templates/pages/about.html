About SecretVault

SecretVault is a secure, privacy-first app for sharing sensitive data via expiring links. Designed for simplicity, speed, and security.

Why SecretVault?

  • One-time access to secrets
  • No permanent storage
  • Simple, fast, and open-source

Tech Stack

  • Go (Fiber) for backend
  • SQLite for lightweight storage
  • Tailwind CSS + DaisyUI for UI

FAQs

How long are secrets stored?

Secrets are automatically deleted after they are viewed or after their expiration time.

Is my data encrypted?

Yes, secrets are stored encrypted in the database until viewed.

Changelog

Privacy Policy

We do not track users or store personal data. All secrets are automatically deleted after use, and no logs are kept. We respect your privacy fully.
